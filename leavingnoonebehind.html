Leaving No One Behind


Leaving No One Behind: Educating Those Most Impacted by AI is my PhD research. I'm researching how education can be used to ensure no one is left behind when technologies, such as AI, advance. The aim of the project is to create an education scheme/framework focusing on people who will be missed by other initiatives (such as in-work retraining, or new university programmes). I am one year into my research. At this stage, I am conducting interviews with a range of thought-leaders, companies and individuals to understand their attitudes to AI and retraining.
